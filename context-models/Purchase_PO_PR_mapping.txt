I want to establish how multiple Purchase receipts can map to a single Purchase Order, how we track the progress of a purchase order
fulfillment and how to track the last purchased price of a product.

1) A purchase order can be fulfilled in multiple deliveries. However, each delivery is accepted with a purchase receipt
So every time, we get a delivery against a PO, it might have all the contents of the PO or partial contents.

For eg:
Purchase Order
H4-fil 100000
H4-met 100000
H4-pack 20000

The supplier gets some of the stuff ready and sends it along with his sales invoice.
We create a Purchase receipt:
H4-fil 70000
H4-met 60000
We create the Purchase receipt and the quantities of item received are credited into the store.

Now, The edit the purchase Order details page-> Order Items section table to have columns: SKU, Item, Qty ordered, Qty received, Qty Pending, Price and Line Total
Where we add item qty received in Purchase receipts to Qty received column.

So now after creating the purchase receipt, the PO looks like:
SKU, Item, Qty ordered, qty received, qty pending, price, line total
H4-fil, H4 Filament, 100000, 70000, 30000, 0.75, 0.75*100000
H4-met, H4 Metal Part, 100000, 60000, 40000, 1.25, 1.25*100000
H4-pack, H4 Packing, 20000, 0, 20000, 0.5, 0.5*20000

Now next time we create a Purchase receipt against this purchase order, we cannot add more qty for an item than the qty pending. 

Also, on the Purchase Order details page, we can see all the purchase receipts that are created against this PO. 
In this show the basic details of the purchase receipt as well and the PR number can be clickable navigating to PR details page.
