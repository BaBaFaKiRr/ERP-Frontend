On GSTR-1 page, give option to user to generate report for an entered time period based on different categories (page layout image attached for reference):
1) HSN wise division. 
A GST is calculated based on the HSN category of an item. For a sales invoice generated, there can be multiple items
having different HSN codes and falling in different tax categories. Thus we collect different tax percentages.
To file the GST at the end of a GST closing period, a business requires the breakup of the items sold grouped by
their HSN code.
For eg, if a company sells:
Item, HSN, Price, Qty
A, 123, 100, 100
B, 122, 120, 102
C, 123, 122, 100

Then, the HSN wise GST summary for this period will have:
HSN: 123
Item, HSN, Qty, Price
A, 123, 100, 100
C, 123, 100, 122

HSN: 122
Item, HSN, Qty, Price
B, 122, 102, 120
